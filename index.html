Bootstrap of 1928 to 2024 annual returns, sampled in 3-year blocks. Assets: S&P 500 total return, 10-year Treasury, Damodaran real estate, gold. Tax: 2025 federal Single brackets, LTCG stacked, Social Security via provisional income, RMDs after age 73. State tax not modeled. Decision rule ranks by success rate, then P10, then median tax, then median terminal. Bootstrap assumes the future return distribution resembles the historical sample.
